How to use:

1. Quick Scan NFC: Stick the card on the NFC Reader of this vending machine.
2. Confirm Personal Details: Review your details on the vending machine screen and confirm to proceed.
3. Insert Your Bottle: Find the designated slot on the vending machine to deposit the bottle.
4. Receive Points: Once the bottle is accepted, the points will be credited to your Sampahmas account.
5. Redeem Points: Use the accumulated points for discounts on the Sampahmas e-commerce platform.
